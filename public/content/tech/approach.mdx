        ## Standard and principles
    </GridColumn>

    <GridColumn width={'3/4'}>
        The service will follow internal NHSBSA, wider government and industry led standards throughout. 

        Internal NHSBSA

        - [NHSBSA Digital, Data and Technology Playbook](/uri "https://nhsbsa.github.io/nhsbsa-digital-playbook/")
        
        NHS and Government
        - [NHS Technology Standards](/uri "https://digital.nhs.uk/about-nhs-digital/corporate-information-and-documents/nhs-digital-data-and-technology-standards")
        - [Government Service Standards](/uri "https://www.gov.uk/service-manual/service-standard")
        - [Government Technology Code of Practice](/uri "https://www.gov.uk/guidance/the-technology-code-of-practice")
        - [Government Cloud First Policy](/uri "https://www.gov.uk/guidance/government-cloud-first-policy")
        - [National Cyber Security Centre Cloud Principles](/uri "https://www.ncsc.gov.uk/collection/cloud/the-cloud-security-principles")
        - [Government standard around making the Technology sustainable](/uri "https://www.gov.uk/guidance/make-your-technology-sustainable")

        Industry
        - [AWS Well Architected Framework](/uri "https://docs.aws.amazon.com/wellarchitected/latest/framework/welcome.html")
        - [OWASP Top 10 Security Risks](/uri "https://owasp.org/www-project-top-ten/")
    </GridColumn>

</GridRow>
