        ## Summary of approach
    </GridColumn>

    <GridColumn width={'3/4'}>

        The team have been organically working towards Beta by validating and investigating user needs and technical options as we've progressed sprint by sprint.

        In sprint 8 we came together for several beta planning workshops. We managed to group features within the service map to understand what features and functionality were Now, Next, and Later (based on value - meeting user needs and outcomes versus effort).

    </GridColumn>

</GridRow>

<GridRow>

    <GridColumn width={'full'}>
        ## Principles of private beta
    </GridColumn>

    <GridColumn width={'3/4'}>

        * A mix of adult social care providers of both size and learning activities.
        * Align as closely as possible with policy rollout and communication.
        * Staged roll-out starting with organisation onboarding and claim submissions.
        * Align with the roll-out of supporting services in the adult social care sector such as the Care Workforce Pathway and Digital Passport.

    </GridColumn>

</GridRow>
