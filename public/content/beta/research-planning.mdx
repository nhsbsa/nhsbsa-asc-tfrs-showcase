        ## Accessibility
    </GridColumn>

    <GridColumn width={'3/4'}>

        We have a plan in place for Private Beta to ensure that the service exceeds expectations when it comes to accessibility. This plan includes:
        - Heuristic reviews, where we (and independent third parties) will judge the design against a set of guidelines (called heuristics) that make systems easy to use
        - Usability sessions either with real users with access needs, or with proxy users. Proxy users will come either from internal accessibility groups, or if need be through a dedicated recruitment agency
        - Seeking feedback from accessibility experts

        We are using the “POUR” guidelines from the Home Office (below) as a starting point. We will also measure our service against Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 to ensure the full accessibility standards are met.
