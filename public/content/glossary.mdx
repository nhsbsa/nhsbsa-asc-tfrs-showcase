        ## Terms and phrases
        <dl>
            <dt>BSA Finance</dt>
            <dd>An employee of the BSA who is responsible for the payment of claims.</dd>
            <dt>BSA Fraud</dt>
            <dd>An employee of the BSA who is responsible for checking 5%-10% of claims post-payment.</dd>
            <dt>BSA Processor</dt>
            <dd>An employee of the BSA who is responsible for processing claims submitted through the service.</dd>
            <dt>Claim</dt>
            <dd>One or more completed line items.</dd>
            <dt>Continuous Professional Development</dt>
            <dd>When referring to CPD we are referring to this in the same way as it is referred to in the Service Commision.</dd>
            <dt>Evidence</dt>
            <dd>Evidence of payment / enrolment / completion.</dd>
            <dt>Inquirer</dt>
            <dd>An employee in an organisation who wishes to determine L&D choices for a learner. (this may based on eligibility and/or TF budget available).</dd>
            <dt>L&D Approver</dt>
            <dd>An employee in an organisation who approves training can be taken by a learner.</dd>
            <dt>L&D Activities</dt>
            <dd>All activities eligible for reimbursement covered under CPD or TU in the service commission.</dd>
            <dt>L&D activity</dt>
            <dd>Learner + Activity + Date.</dd>
            <dt>Learner</dt>
            <dd>An employee in an organisation who participated in an L&D activity and who is referred to in the learner activity as part of the claim.</dd>
            <dt>Line Item</dt>
            <dd>Learning & Development (L&D) activity + evidence.</dd>
            <dt>Organisation</dt>
            <dd>An employer who is eligible to use the TFRS eg would pass the requirements to create a S4C ASC-WDS account.</dd>
            <dt>Organisation Finance</dt>
            <dd>An employee in an organisation who is responsible for reconciling finances associated with training expenditure.</dd>
            <dt>Parent Organisation</dt>
            <dd>A larger organisation made up of multiple smaller organisations.</dd>
            <dt>Submitter</dt>
            <dd>An employee in an organisation who is approved to submit claims for that organisation.</dd>
            <dt>Targeted Upskilling</dt>
            <dd>When referring to TU we are referring to this in the same way as it is referred to in the Service Commision.</dd>
            <dt>Training provider</dt>
            <dd>An organisation that provides ASC training.</dd>
        </dl>
    </GridColumn>
